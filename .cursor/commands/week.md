## Common Scenarios

**Scenario: Week derailed by fire drill**
- Document what happened
- Identify root cause
- Add preventive action to next week
- Communicate to stakeholders

**Scenario: Consistently not meeting commitments**
- Review weekly retrospectives for patterns
- Check: Are we over-committing?
- Use prioritization: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/2.1.2-Strategic-Execution/4-Prioritization
- Adjust team capacity expectations

**Scenario: Team morale dropping**
- Increase frequency of wins celebration
- Check workload balance
- 1-on-1s with team members
- Reference team dynamics: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.0-Foundations/2.0.1-Mental-Models/5-Team-Dynamics

**Scenario: Stakeholders complaining about lack of updates**
- Send Friday update consistently
- Add stakeholders to update list
- Use newsletter framework: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.4-Communication/2.4.1-Newsletter
