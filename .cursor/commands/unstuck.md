# Unstuck Task

Diagnostic tool to identify where you're blocked and suggest specific solutions. Maps symptoms to root causes, then recommends frameworks and actions.

## Diagnostic Questions

Answer these to identify your block:

### About Your Situation
- What specifically feels stuck or unclear?
- How long have you been stuck on this?
- What have you already tried?
- What's the impact of staying stuck?

### Symptom Categories
Which best describes your situation?

**A) Direction unclear** - Don't know what to build or why
**B) Too many priorities** - Everything feels urgent, nothing moves forward
**C) No customer insight** - Building in the dark, no validation
**D) Stakeholder misalignment** - Politics, conflicting opinions, no buy-in
**E) Execution chaos** - Busy but not productive, constantly firefighting
**F) Decision paralysis** - Can't decide, too many options or unknowns
**G) Team dysfunction** - Blockers, unclear ownership, low morale

## Diagnosis & Solutions

### A) DIRECTION UNCLEAR

**Root Cause:** Missing or weak strategy

**Symptoms:**
- Reactive feature requests without clear purpose
- Can't explain "why" to stakeholders
- Team builds features that don't connect to outcomes
- Constantly changing priorities

**Solutions:**

**Immediate (This Week):**
1. Run /bias command - Check for confirmation bias, bandwagon effect
2. Braindump: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/2.1.1-Strategic-Foundations
3. Use pre-mortem: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.0-Foundations/2.0.1-Mental-Models/1-Decision-Making/1-pre-mortems.md

**Next 2 Weeks:**
- Strategic foundations: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/2.1.1-Strategic-Foundations
- Set OKRs: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/2.1.2-Strategic-Execution/1-OKR
- North Star metric: @02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/2.1.2-Strategic-Execution/3-North-Star

**Related:** Use /start if beginning new initiative

If you have multiple symptoms from different categories:
