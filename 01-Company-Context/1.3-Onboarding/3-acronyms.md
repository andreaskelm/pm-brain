## Acronyms and shorthand (quick reference)

This is a lightweight lexicon for the acronyms and shorthand you will see across PM Brain and your company context. It is intentionally generic so you can adapt it to your real organization.

When an acronym appears in a company or initiative doc and you are not sure what it means, check here first, then follow the link to the canonical framework or template.

### Core PM and product acronyms

| Acronym | Expansion | What it refers to | Canonical doc / folder |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|------------------------|
| OKR | Objectives and Key Results | Goal-setting system that connects strategy to measurable outcomes | `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/2.1.2-Strategic-Execution/1-OKR/` |
| PRD | Product Requirements Document | Structured doc describing a problem, context, and solution shape for an initiative | `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.3-Execution/2.3.4-PRD/` |
| JTBD | Jobs To Be Done | Framework for understanding customer jobs, pains, and gains | See JTBD and discovery methods under `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.2-Discovery/` (adapt path to your setup) |
| NSM | North Star Metric | Single (or small set of) metric(s) that best capture long-term value creation | See strategy and metrics methods under `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/` and `2.9-Other/` |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score | Survey-based loyalty and recommendation metric | See communication and research methods under `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.3-Execution/` and `2.4-Communication/` |
| CSAT | Customer Satisfaction | Short survey measuring satisfaction with a product or interaction | See support/experience-related methods in `02-Methods-and-Tools/` as you add them |
| LTV | Lifetime Value | Estimated total value (revenue or margin) you earn from a customer over time | See strategy and pricing discussions under `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/` |
| CAC | Customer Acquisition Cost | All-in cost to acquire a customer | Used alongside LTV in strategy and growth discussions; see `02-Methods-and-Tools/2.1-Strategy/` |
