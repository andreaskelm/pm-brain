# Product-Market Fit Assessment Templates

## Introduction

This document provides ready-to-use templates for assessing product-market fit. Choose the template that fits your needs: Quick PMF Check for gut assessment, PMF Survey for quantitative validation, or Full PMF Assessment for comprehensive analysis.

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Quick PMF Check**: Use for gut-check assessment (5-10 minutes)
2. **PMF Survey**: Use for standardized PMF measurement (monthly/quarterly)
3. **Full PMF Assessment**: Use for comprehensive PMF analysis (quarterly)

## How to Maintain

- **Weekly**: Track retention and growth metrics
- **Monthly**: Quick PMF check and user interviews
- **Every 4-6 weeks (Pre-PMF)**: Run PMF survey
- **Quarterly (Post-PMF)**: Full PMF assessment
