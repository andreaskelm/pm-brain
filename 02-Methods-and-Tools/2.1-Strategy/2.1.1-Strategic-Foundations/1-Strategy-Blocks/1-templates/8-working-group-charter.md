## 1. Strategy Working Group Charter

### Team Assembly & Roles

**Core Team (Required - Minimum 4 people):**

| Role | Responsibilities | Time Commitment | Key Deliverables |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Product Manager** (Owner) | Process facilitation, stakeholder alignment, document writing | 8-12 hrs/week | Leadership interviews, final strategy document, rollout execution |
| **Engineering Lead** | Technical constraints, feasibility assessment, implementation perspective | 3-5 hrs/week | Technical constraints analysis, pillar feasibility input |
| **Design Lead** | UX insights synthesis, design sprint leadership, concept creation | 4-6 hrs/week | UX research synthesis, design sprint facilitation, illustrative concepts |
| **Data Lead** | Behavioral analysis, metrics baseline, measurement framework | 3-5 hrs/week | Behavioral insights pack, baseline metrics, success measurement framework |

**Extended Team (Optional - Add if available):**

| Role | Responsibilities | Time Commitment | Key Deliverables |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Product Marketing** | Competitive analysis, market positioning, messaging | 2-4 hrs/week | Competitive heatmap, positioning analysis |
| **User Research** | Research synthesis, user observation coordination | 2-4 hrs/week | UXR insights synthesis, scrappy study execution |
| **Content Design** | Communication clarity, documentation support | 1-2 hrs/week | Document editing, communication templates |

### Meeting Cadence

**Preparation Phase (Weeks 1-4):**
- Weekly check-ins (30 min) - progress updates and blocker resolution
- Bi-weekly working sessions (60 min) - collaborative deliverable review

**Strategy Sprint Week:**
- Daily commitment: 9 AM - 5 PM, Days 1-3 (flexible Days 4-5)
- All core team members required, extended team as available

**Document Writing Phase:**
- Weekly reviews (30 min) - PM shares drafts, team provides feedback
- Final review session (90 min) - complete document walkthrough

**Decision-Making Process:**
- **Consensus preferred** for strategic decisions (pillar selection)
- **PM has final call** on process decisions and document content
- **Escalation path:** Direct manager → VP of Product → Leadership team
