# Playing to Win Templates

## Introduction

This document provides ready-to-use templates for building strategy using the Playing to Win Strategic Choice Cascade framework. Use these templates with the framework guide (`playing-to-win.md`) to make strategic choices about markets, positioning, and capabilities.

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Strategic Choice Cascade Template**: Use to answer all 5 strategic questions and build your strategy
2. **Coherence Check**: Use to validate that your choices work together

---

## Strategic Choice Cascade Template

```
# [Product/Company] Strategic Choice Cascade

## 1. Winning Aspiration
**What winning looks like:**
[Clear statement of ultimate outcome you're trying to achieve]

**For whom:**
[Specific stakeholders/customers]

**Why this matters:**
[Connection to mission/vision]

---

## 2. Where Will We Play?

**Geography:**
- Will play: [Specific markets]
- Will NOT play: [Excluded markets]

**Product/Service:**
- Will offer: [Specific products]
- Will NOT offer: [Excluded products]

**Customer Segment:**
- Will serve: [Specific segments]
- Will NOT serve: [Excluded segments]

**Distribution Channel:**
- Will use: [Specific channels]
- Will NOT use: [Excluded channels]

---

## 3. How Will We Win?

**Value proposition:**
[Specific way you'll create superior value in chosen playing field]

**Competitive advantage:**
[What you can do that competitors can't/won't]

**Why customers will choose us:**
[Specific customer benefits]

---

## 4. Core Capabilities Required

**Must-have capabilities:**
1. [Capability]: [Why critical to "how to win"]
2. [Capability]: [Why critical to "how to win"]
3. [Capability]: [Why critical to "how to win"]

**Current state:**
- Have: [Existing capabilities]
- Need to build: [Capability gaps]
- Need to acquire: [Partner/buy]

**Priority actions:**
[Top 3 capability-building initiatives]

---

## 5. Management Systems Required

**Measurement:**
- Key metrics: [Specific measures of winning]
- Dashboards: [Links to tracking systems]

**Operating processes:**
- Decision-making: [How strategic choices get made]
- Resource allocation: [How budget/people are assigned]

**Accountability:**
- Owners: [Who owns each strategic element]
- Review cadence: [How often we assess]

**Incentives:**
[How we reward strategic execution]

---

## Coherence Check

**Vertical coherence:**
- [ ] "Where" supports "aspiration"
- [ ] "How" works in chosen "where"
- [ ] "Capabilities" enable chosen "how"
- [ ] "Systems" support "capabilities"

**Horizontal coherence:**
- [ ] No contradictions across choices
- [ ] No gaps in logic
- [ ] No conflicts in execution

**Revision notes:**
[Document any changes made during integration]
```
