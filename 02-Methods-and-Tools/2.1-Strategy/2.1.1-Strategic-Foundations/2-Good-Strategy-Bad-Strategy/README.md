# Good Strategy/Bad Strategy - The Kernel

**Framework by:** Richard Rumelt (UCLA Anderson School of Management)  
**Published:** Book - Good Strategy Bad Strategy (2011)  
**What it is:** Strategy evaluation framework (Diagnosis → Guiding Policy → Coherent Actions)

## Files
- `good-strategy-bad-strategy.md` — Complete Kernel methodology and evaluation guide
- `2-good-strategy-bad-strategy-template.md` — Ready-to-use templates (Evaluation checklist and Building Strategy template)

## When to Use
- Evaluating if your strategy is "good"
- Diagnosing why current strategy isn't working
- Teaching teams what good strategy looks like
