# One-Pager Templates

## Overview

This document contains ready-to-use templates for different types of one-pagers. Each template follows the universal structure defined in the framework and can be customized for your specific needs.

**Before using templates:**
1. Review `1-one-pager-framework.md` to understand principles and best practices
2. Choose the appropriate template type for your situation
3. Customize the template to your specific context
4. Review against the quality checklist before sharing

-----

## Template Usage Tips

1. **Start with the TL;DR** - Write this last, but put it first. It should capture everything important.

2. **Customize for your audience** - Executives need different detail than cross-functional teams.

3. **Use data liberally** - Numbers are more persuasive than words.

4. **Make the ask explicit** - Don't make readers guess what you need.

5. **Test before sharing** - Have one person from your target audience review it first.

6. **Keep it scannable** - Use formatting, bullets, and whitespace to make it easy to skim.
