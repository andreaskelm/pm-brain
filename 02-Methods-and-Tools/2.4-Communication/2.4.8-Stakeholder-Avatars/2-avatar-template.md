# Avatar template (one person)

Save as a new file in [01-Company-Context/1.1-Stakeholder-Avatars/](../../../01-Company-Context/1.1-Stakeholder-Avatars/README.md) with filename `N-name-role.md` (single digit, name, role; optional organization). Examples: `1-jane-manager.md`, `2-alex-eng-lead.md`, `3-sam-design-lead-acme.md`. See that folder's README for the naming convention. Keep the headings exactly as below so agents can parse the structure.

```markdown
### [Name] — [Role / Organization]

#### Identity & Relationship
- **Organization position**: [Where they sit in the organization, reporting lines relevant to you]
- **Time working together**: [Rough duration]
- **Relationship summary**: [One sentence: relationship right now]

#### Explicit Goals (What they say they care about)
- [Goal 1 – in their words if possible]
- [Goal 2]
- [Goal 3]

#### Implicit Goals (What they might not say out loud)
- [Hypothesis about career goals, status, territory, legacy, etc.]
- [Where they may feel insecure or threatened]

#### Fears & Risks
- [Events or outcomes they are afraid could land badly on them]
- [Incidents they'd see as career-limiting or reputationally risky]

#### Incentives & Levers
- **What moves them to support something:**
  - [Evidence, narratives, sponsors, metrics, user stories, etc.]
- **Evidence that changed their mind in the past:**
  - [Concrete example(s)]

#### Tone of Voice & Behaviour
- **In groups:** [How they show up in group settings]
- **In 1:1s:** [Differences from group behavior]
- **Style:** [Blunt vs indirect, optimistic vs cautious, detail vs big-picture, etc.]
- **Example phrases:**
  - "[Real phrase they use]"
  - "[Real phrase they use]"
  - "[Real phrase they use]"

#### History With You
- **Backed you on:**
  - [Examples where they supported your work]
- **Blocked / slowed you on:**
  - [Examples where they resisted or slowed things down]
- **Unresolved tension or scars:**
  - [Any lingering issues from past incidents]

#### Brainfeed Stream (How they'd react)
- **Good news scenario:**
  - **Out loud:** [What they'd likely say]
  - **Inner monologue:** [What they'd think but not say]
- **Bad news or surprise change:**
  - **Out loud:** [What they'd likely say]
  - **Inner monologue:** [What they'd think but not say]
```
