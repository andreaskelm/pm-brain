# Meeting Agenda Templates

## Overview

This document contains ready-to-use agenda templates for different types of meetings. Each template follows best practices defined in the framework and can be customized for your specific context.

**Before using templates:**
1. Review `1-meeting-agendas-framework.md` to understand meeting principles and best practices
2. Choose the appropriate meeting type for your goal
3. Customize the template to your specific context
4. Share agenda 24 hours in advance with pre-read materials

-----

## Template Usage Tips

1. **Customize for your context** - No template fits perfectly; adapt as needed
2. **Share in advance** - Send agenda 24 hours before with any pre-reads
3. **Timebox strictly** - Respect the time allocations
4. **Assign roles** - Notetaker, facilitator, timekeeper
5. **Follow up** - Share notes within 24 hours with action items
